How it works

From comment to conversion
in seconds

A four-step pipeline runs silently in the background across all your connected platforms.

1

Comments captured in real time

Convale monitors your connected social accounts continuously — Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube — pulling every new comment the moment it lands.

2

AI classifies and encodes intent

Each comment is analyzed and encoded — flagged as a sale inquiry, engagement, or spam — with a confidence score attached to every classification.

3

The right response is formed and decoded

Encoded intent is decoded into a tailored reply — conversion-focused for buyers, retention-focused for fans, silent suppression for spam.

4

You see every outcome in your dashboard

Track lead capture rate, response volume, and comment breakdown by category. Understand your audience at a glance — and never lose a lead again.
